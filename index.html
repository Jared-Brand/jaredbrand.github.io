Jared Brand

My name is Jared Brand and I am a passionate Student Surveyor in Melbourne, Australia. I am finishing my Bachelor's Degree with Honours this year and am excited to begin my full-time career at Spiire Australia Pty Ltd! I am an enthusiastic person who loves to take on new and exciting challenges, as it gives me an opportunity to display my knowledge and problem-solving skills! Have a look at some of my awesome projects.

Through my time and teachings of both University and working at Spiire since 2019, I have been able to develop many abilities to benefit myself. I have learned what it takes to be a hard worker and what it means to work as a team through group projects and jobs that require multiple workers! These opportunities at RMIT and Spiire have helped me to develop my knowledge about Surveying and how to really give it my all to every project that is given to me. No matter how big or small the challenge is, you can be assured that I will give my absolute best to deliver an efficient and accurate result to the client, whatever the case may be.

Project 1

One of the most rewarding Projects I have worked on is the Newhaven Estate located in Tarneit, Victoria. This Project has currently delivered 17 Stages of Development and will be completing another 10 Stages over the next couple of years. Newhaven Estate have also just got the go-ahead to begin construction in a new Practice Area, meaning there will be another 600 Properties developed on top of this!

The Newhaven Estate has required me to complete many different types of surveys. I have been required to compete large-scale Feature & Level Surveys as well as Title Re-Establishment Surveys which must be completed on an open farmland area before construction can begin. I have also completed many Control Surveys which allow Spiire and other Surveyors to adopt these Survey Marks to complete their works. One of the most common pieces of work I must complete are Asset Recording Surveys. Asset Recording Surveys allow me to determine if any Sewer, Water and Drainage Pits and Pipes have been built correctly, which requires me to be very well organised with a high level of communication between myself, engineers and the Contractors!

Project 2

Another very rewarding Project I completed for Spiire was a Wetlands Survey performed at the Manor Lakes Estate located in Wyndham Vale! This required myself and another Surveyor to safely enter the bottom of several creeks and determine the depths at the base. Completing these surveys meant that the risks of Injury were higher than usual, so an in-depth Task Briefing was performed before this job was completed and we collectively discussed the most safe way of completing this task, which worked effectively on site as there were no injuries.

I re-visited this site approximately 12 months after this survey was performed, and the look and aesthetic of the area was overwhelming and wonderful to see that our work had gone a long way to creating something amazing.
